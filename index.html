About Me

My name is Zaihidin Bin Abdul Rahman, and I living in Kuching, Sarawak. I hold a Bachelor's degree with Honors in Information Technology from Open University Malaysia. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Information Technology Management (MITM) at UNIMAS. Currently, I work as an Assistant Security Officer at the Sarawak Security and Enforcement Unit (SSEU) under Jabatan Premier Sarawak.

Throughout my career, I have served as an Investigation Officer, Prosecution Officer, and Operational Technical Enforcement Officer which is specializing in delegated power in accordance with Sarawak Ordinances. Besides that, I am also certified as a Rocheston Certified Cybersecurity Specialist (RCCS) and Rocheston Certified Cybersecurity Engineer (RCCE). Looking forward, I am eager to expand my expertise as a Cybersecurity Engineer in Sarawak Civil Service (SCS).

Outside of my professional endeavors, I am a main vocalist in the Gladiator Band and have had the privilege of performing at corporate dinners. These experiences provide a unique aspect to my character and enrich my interactions both within and outside of my professional environment.
